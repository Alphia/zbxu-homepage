Machine Learning Group

Mainly focuses on characterizing and modeling of sparse one-dimensional vectors, two-dimensional matrices, high-dimensional tensors and their related computation and real-word applications. The major research topics include: noncovex regularization theory, T-sparsity modeling, sparse SAR Imaging, low-rank decompositions with applications in information processing.
